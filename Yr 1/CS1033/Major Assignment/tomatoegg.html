Tomato Egg Stir Fry

Is proof that we can have our egg, garlic, tomato and rice cake and eat it too. It is fast. It is easy. Best of all? It is tasty!

In my opinion most recipes for this can be summed up as:

  • Start rice cooker.
  • Cut up some tomatoes, garlic and green onion.
  • Crack some eggs.
  • Basically make scrambled eggs in a big pot.
  • Add veggies to scrambled eggs.
  • Stir fry some more.
  • Wait for rice to finish.
  • Enjoy!

With the right proportions and a bit of practice it is a quick and beautifully tasty blend of tomato and egg. Even if only sauce is left over it can be used over plain rice to achieve a rice +9 which heal 9 health points and gives +9 luck for 10 seconds.
