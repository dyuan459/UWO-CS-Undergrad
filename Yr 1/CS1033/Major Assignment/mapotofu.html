Mapo Tofu!!!

Mapo tofu is the best. It is spicy, has protein in tofu (and meat I guess), tasty and hard to get wrong.

Personally, I believe the "ma" in mapo should be emphasized while cooking in true Sichuan spirit. The "ma" being Chinese for roughly "numbing", maybe even a tingly numb. The only possible problem with mapo tofu is that it cannot be cooked with soft tofu (and maybe how oily it can be).

All in all, you should try it, maybe at a restaurant or maybe with a recipe. Well, not should. There is no option. I have cursed you. You must have mapo tofu in 32872.5 days or I will fill your kettle with mapo tofu.

Well what are you waiting for? Try some,

NOW!!!

  

Anatomy of a Superior Mapo Tofu

Although I say now and although all mapo tofu is perfect, some things do make certain kinds of mapo tofu stand above the rest. Here are some key terms for a superior mapo tofu:

  • Spicy but it should not be the focus.
  • A balance of tofu and meat to sauce of roughly 4:6. Like there should be somewhat more sauce than meat and tofu.
  • Sauce is important because it enhances rice into at least rice +10 with the added effects upon consumption of: -10 health points, +10 attack power with a slight chance of granting a +10 on their next attack roll.
  • Numbing/"ma"/"tingly numb" should be the focus of mapo tofu! If I can feel my mouth after eating the mapo tofu should it really be called mapo tofu?! 
  • Green onions. It must be added. Steal from your family if you must. No good mapo tofu is complete without green onions!
